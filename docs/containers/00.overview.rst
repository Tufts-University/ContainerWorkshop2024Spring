Welcome to Containers @ Tufts Research Technology
=================================================

In this section, we will introduce containers, explain their uses, and discuss different container technologies. Our focus will be on two specific technologies: `Docker <https://www.docker.com>`_ and `SingularityCE <https://sylabs.io/singularity/>`_/`Apptainer <https://apptainer.org>`_.

**Objectives for this session:**

  * What are containers?
  * Why should we use containers?
  * Introduction to various container technologies.
